====== Devotions ======
Created Wednesday 19 April 2023

@gameplay @lore @dev @body @mind @soul 

Devotions are the most important part of [[Classes|class]] selection, they consist of Body, Mind and Soul, each one having different properties.
During class selection, players can choose one of them, and it will determine their class in conjunction with their choice of [[Dedications|Dedication.]]
Devotions have a type of animal associated with them. 
Devotions represent the alingment of their intentions, wether they are athletic, intelligent or soulful.
