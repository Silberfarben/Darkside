====== Mind ======
Created Thursday 20 April 2023

@dev @lore @gameplay @mind 

-Mind
Mind is a devotion that focuses on mental skills, dealing and avoiding damage with planning and intelligence, as well as rationing their resources, being sneaky and avoiding confrontations. 
Mind is assosiated with insects, and its matching Dedication is [[Dedications:Cunning|Cunning]]
Mind classes are Rogue classes.
